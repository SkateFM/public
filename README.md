# 🛼 Skate.FM | Preservation through Performance

![Skate.FM Banner](https://github.com/SkateFM/skate.fm/raw/main/public/shop_hero_skates.png?text=SKATE.FM+-+CENTRAL+FLORIDA+ROLLER+RADIO)

## 📡 The Mission
**Skate.FM** is a non-profit digital broadcast and archival platform dedicated to the preservation of roller skating culture. We focus on the high-energy foundations of the rink experience: Rink Classics, Miami Bass, Freestyle, and Old School music. Based in Central Florida, our mission is to provide a global stage for the music and the moves that define the hardwood.

## 💎 The Vision
We believe in the power of authentic, human-created music and art. In an era of algorithmic noise, Skate.FM serves as a curated sanctuary for the skating community, blending modern high-performance technology with the soul of the 80s retro and synthwave aesthetic.

---

## 🛠 Our Platform
The Skate.FM ecosystem is built on a high-performance stack designed for stability and broadcast excellence:

* **Frontend:** [React 19](https://react.dev/) deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) for lightning-fast delivery.
* **Backend:** [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/) for real-time listener engagement and metadata synchronization.
* **Infrastructure:** Enterprise-grade [Proxmox](https://www.proxmox.com/) virtualization running on AMD Ryzen hardware.
* **Broadcast:** Fine-tuned [Icecast](https://icecast.org/) streams optimized for stability across mobile and desktop environments.
